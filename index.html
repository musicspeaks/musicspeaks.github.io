Our Mission

In many public schools there are no music programs as they do not have musical instruments and teachers. Our Goal is to collect instruments and send them to Venezuela to schools and orchestras for children to develop their musical skills. We believe that Music develops the brain and empowers the imagination. Many people have instruments in their houses that they do no longer use. Our club would collect those instruments and send them to Venezuela.

Where words fail, music speaks.
