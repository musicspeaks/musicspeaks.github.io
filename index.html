Our Mission

In many public schools there are not music programs as they not have instruments and teachers. Our Goal is to collect instruments and send them to Venezuela to schools and orchestras for children to develop their musical skills. We believe that Music develops the brain and empowers the imagination. Many people have instruments in their houses have music instruments that they do no longer us use. Our club would collect those instruments and send them to a public school.

Where words fail, music speaks.
